The profits shown above are for optimal hardware configurations. NiceHash rentability is negative for most coins, indicating that direct mining is currently more profitable than renting hashrate for these algorithms.

## RTX 3080 Ti Specific:
- Supports all major algorithms (Ethash, KawPow, Octopus, Autolykos2, etc.)
- 12GB VRAM allows mining most coins (except some with higher DAG requirements)
- Power efficiency is key to profitability
