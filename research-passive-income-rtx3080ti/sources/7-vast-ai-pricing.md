## RTX 3080 Ti Pricing on Vast.ai
- **Median Rate:** $0.09/hr
- **Range:** $0.03 - $0.80/hr
- VRAM: 12GB Ampere

## Host Earnings Potential (from Reddit discussions):
- $0.30/hr when rented (older data)
- Potential: $2600/year if constantly rented (older estimate)
- ~$23/day reported by one user with a rig
- ~$600-700/month possible with consistent utilization

## Key Considerations:
- Vast.ai requires Linux (better for dedicated rigs)
- Not ideal for desktop sharing like Salad
- Per-second billing for renters
- Host payouts: 2 weeks to first payout, then weekly on Fridays
- Demand has dropped significantly in 2024-2025
- GPU rental market is competitive

## Platform Comparison from Community:
- Vast.ai: Lower prices, requires Linux, better for dedicated equipment
- Salad: Easier for desktop users, can pause/resume
- Clore.ai: Alternative with CLORE token rewards
- Runpod: More reliable but more expensive

## Market Reality Check:
- YouTube reports: ~$23/day average on a rig (Red Panda Mining)
